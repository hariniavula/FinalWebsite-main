Visualization 4: Purchase Amount by Gender & Age Group

Description

This stacked bar chart visualizes total purchase amounts segmented by gender and age group in Massachusetts. The chart chart uses bars as its marks. The channels include position along the x-axis to distinguish between gender categories (Male and Female), and length or position along the y-axis to encode total purchase amount in USD. There is also a color channel to differentiate between age groups.


Interactive Features

There are radio buttons to toggle between viewing all age groups or specific age ranges (0-10, 11-20, 21-30, etc.).


Key Takeaways

This figure shows spending patterns across different demographics in Massachusetts, showing how purchase behavior varies by gender and age group. Men consistently outspend women across every age category. The highest spending demographic is men aged 61-70, while women aged 51-60 represent the lowest spending group.
